Wildlife Habitat Loss

Habitat Loss & Wildlife

Millions of animals lose their homes each year due to deforestation, urbanization, and agriculture. Iconic species like elephants, tigers, and orangutans are increasingly endangered.

Poaching

Poaching & Illegal Hunting

Animals are hunted for fur, ivory, horns, and meat. This drastically reduces populations and destabilizes ecosystems.

Pollution

Pollution

Plastics, chemicals, and waste contaminate rivers, oceans, and land. Animals ingest toxins or get trapped in debris, threatening their survival.

Stray Cats & Dogs

Globally, there are **hundreds of millions of stray cats and dogs** living on the streets. Many die from hunger, disease, accidents, or extreme weather. In some countries, **thousands are euthanized each year** to control overpopulation.

Spaying and neutering, vaccination programs, and responsible pet ownership are crucial to reduce suffering. Awareness campaigns and community initiatives can save countless lives.
