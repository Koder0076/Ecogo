Main Activities

How Ecogo brings ecology closer to people

School Eco-Contests

A competition between schools where students create eco-projects, posters, and campaigns to protect animals and nature, competing for the best environmental project aimed at solving ecological problems.

Environmental Events

Organization of sports events, ecological clean-ups, and outdoor campaigns that unite communities in action.

Lectures and Workshops

Interactive lectures in schools and kindergartens where children learn about ecology through stories and play.

Kindergarten Visits

Games and creative workshops for children that show how important and fun it is to care for animals and nature from an early age.

Team Initiatives

Collaboration with schools, volunteers, and organizations to develop community eco-spirit and teamwork.
